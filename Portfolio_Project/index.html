Gughan Shelvaraj

Software Engineer

Here's who I am and What I do

With five years of experience in backend software engineering, I have developed a solid foundation in building scalable, efficient, and maintainable systems. Over the course of my career, I’ve gained extensive knowledge in backend technologies, including proficiency in languages such as Java, Python, and Node.js. My expertise extends to designing and implementing RESTful APIs, working with microservices architectures, and ensuring that the systems I build can handle high levels of traffic while maintaining optimal performance. I have hands-on experience with relational and NoSQL databases such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB, allowing me to efficiently store and query data while ensuring data integrity and security.

I’ve also worked closely with DevOps teams, streamlining the deployment process through automation, CI/CD pipelines, and containerization using Docker and Kubernetes. Understanding the full lifecycle of software from development to deployment allows me to take a proactive approach to troubleshooting and performance optimization. Throughout my career, I’ve been committed to writing clean, readable, and well-documented code, following best practices such as test-driven development (TDD) and ensuring robust unit and integration tests.

Collaboration has been key to my success, as I consistently work alongside front-end developers, product managers, and other engineers to deliver high-quality solutions on time. I am comfortable in agile environments, adapting quickly to shifting priorities while maintaining a strong focus on problem-solving and delivering value to customers. My strong communication skills have allowed me to convey technical concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders effectively. As I continue to evolve in my career, I am passionate about exploring new technologies and methodologies to refine my craft and contribute to building innovative software solutions.
